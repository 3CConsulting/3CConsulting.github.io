About Us

3C Consulting is a student-run consultancy that works with local private and public businesses in the National Capital Region. We focus on helping small businesses grow to their potential by developing solutions based on optimization and integration of the businesses’ functioning areas. 3C Consulting is uniquely positioned to provide a low cost comprehensive solution to local clients while providing both fresh academic perspectives and immediate support throughout the implementation.
