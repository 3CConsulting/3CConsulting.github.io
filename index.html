About Us

3C Consulting is a student-run consultancy that works with local private and public businesses in the National Capital Region. We focus on helping small businesses grow to their potential by developing solutions based on optimization and integration of the businesses’ functioning areas. 3C Consulting is uniquely positioned to provide a low cost comprehensive solution to local clients while providing both fresh academic perspectives and immediate support throughout the implementation.

Our Values

Competence: 3C Consultants have many industry experiences in the sector of IT, finance, operations and marketing. See individual profiles for detailed descriptions.

Commitment: Each project is handled with close monitoring and progress is regularly updated. In-depth before-after analysis will be provided to measure the effectiveness of the solution.

Community: 3C Consulting believes in giving back to the supportive community and will take on at least one pro bono project every year.
